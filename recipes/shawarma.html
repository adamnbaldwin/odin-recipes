Adam's Yummy Chicken Shawarma

Description

This simple but delicious recipe will satisfy your guests

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • Cumin

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine chicken, olive oil, lemon juice, and cumin. Marinate for at least 2 hours and up to overnight.
  2. In an oven preheated to 450 degrees F, cook chicken for 20 minutes until brown around edges.
