Adam's Yummy Tsaziki

Description

This simple put delicious recipe will satisfy your guests

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 persian cucumbers
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. On the small side of a box grater, grate the cucumbers. Pat dry with paper towel.
  2. Combine cucumber with yogurt, lemon juice, and salt an pepper to taste. Serve immediately.
