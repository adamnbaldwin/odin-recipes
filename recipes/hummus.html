Adam's Yummy Hummus

Description

This simple but delicious recipe will satisfy your guests

Ingredients

  • 1 can Garbanzo beans
  • 2 Tbsp tahini paste
  • Lemon juice
  • Icewater

Steps

  1. In a food processor, blend garbanzo beans and tahini paste until combined into a rough paste
  2. Add lemon juice and water and pulse until consistency is smooth
